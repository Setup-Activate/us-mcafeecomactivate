Consumer Pre-Installation Tool

If your installation is incomplete on your Windows device for some reason, try to use McAfee’s Pre-Install Tool for resolving your issue. This tool gets your PC ready for installation. For learning to download as well as run the Pre-Install tool, follow the steps given below:

  1. First, download the Pre-Install Tool. Save the recently downloaded file to a temporary location, like on your desktop.
  2. Double-click on the Pre-Install_Tool.exe, and do as the prompts direct you.
  3. Read through the warning message thoroughly, and then click on OK upon agreeing to all the changes.
  4. After the downloading part of the Preinstall Tool is done:
    1. Firstly, click on OK.
    2. Then, click on Close.
    3. Finally, reboot your computer see if the file runs.
  5. Try installing your product once again.

Run Windows Disk Defragmenter

Defragmenting your local hard drives makes the performance better.

NOTE: If in place of a mechanical (spinning) hard drive, you have a Solid-State Disk (SSD, you do not require a defragmenter.

To run the defragmenter:

Click on the “Start” button on the lower left of the taskbar.

Type “defragment” in the search box, and then press Enter.

  • On Windows version 8.x and 10.x: Click on “Defragment and Optimize Drives”.
  • On Windows version  7.x: Click on “Disk Defragmenter”.

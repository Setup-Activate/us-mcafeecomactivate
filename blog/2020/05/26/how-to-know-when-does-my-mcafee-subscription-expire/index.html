Steps to check McAfee subscription

So how exactly can you know when your McAfee subscription is set to expire? Here is a guide to help you get that information with ease so you can always keep your system protected from any and all online and local threats:

Check on your PC

  • The very first thing you need to do is to verify your subscription on your PC.
  • To do this, you need to launch the www.mcAfee.com/activate application on your computer by clicking on the icon in your Taskbar tray.
  • Locate and click on the option that says Verify Subscription.
  • Click on Yes when asked to give permission to the app to make changes to your device.
  • Once the process is complete, check the subscription information and confirm if it is accurate.

Check on McAfee Activate website

  • In the case that the above-mentioned way does not work or there is an issue with the subscription information shown, you can fix it by McAfee subscription repair tool find it through the McAfee website.
  • Head to home.McAfee.com and Sign In with your existing credentials.
  • Once you have logged in, go to My Account.
  • Here, select the computer or device you wish to check the expiration date for.
  • Your subscription information for that device will be displayed, including the expiration date.
  • You can also find the expiry date of the McAfee product in the My Info tab on your McAfee software on the PC.
  • You should always verify that the expiration date of the product on your account matches with the one shown on the software.
